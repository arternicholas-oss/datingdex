7 DC Date Night Mistakes You're Definitely Making (And How to Fix Them)

Mistake 1: Suggesting the Wharf on a Saturday

It'll be slammed. Parking is a nightmare. You'll spend date one yelling over a cover band. Go Tuesday.

Mistake 2: Booking 9 PM for a first date

First dates should end before exhaustion sets in. 7:00 or 7:30 is the sweet spot. You can always extend. You can never un-extend.

Mistake 3: The surprise dress code

If the restaurant has a dress code, TELL THEM. Nothing ends a date faster than watching your date realize everyone around them is in blazers.

Mistake 4: Picking a chain "to be safe"

Chains are not safe. Chains are boring. Boring is a red flag. DC has 94 great independent spots in the DatingDex directory. Use it.

Mistake 5: Not making a reservation

"Let's just walk in" is a personality disorder on a first date. Book it.

Mistake 6: Splitting the check weirdly

Just split it or offer to pay. The calculator app on a first date is the single worst vibe there is.

Mistake 7: Ignoring what they said they like

If they mentioned they love wine, pick a wine bar. If they're vegetarian, don't book a steakhouse. Listening is free.
