Georgetown Date Ideas: Beyond the Cupcake Tourist Trap

Georgetown is beautiful. Georgetown is historic. Georgetown is also, on any Saturday, absolutely overrun with people doing The Cupcake Thing. You do not need to do The Cupcake Thing.

Start at the C&O Canal

Walk the towpath for 20 minutes. It's shaded, quiet, and makes you look like a person who appreciates infrastructure history. (You don't have to actually appreciate it.)

Dinner at Fiola Mare

Riverfront, elevated, not pretentious despite all evidence to the contrary. Full review →

Or: casual at The Berliner

Beer garden, pretzels, waterfront, low stakes. If your date orders a stein, that's commitment.

Drinks at The Gibson (technically 14th St but close enough)

Hidden speakeasy energy, excellent cocktails, looks like you put effort in. Why it works →

The Move: bookstore flirting

There's a specific category of date where you wander a bookstore together and learn each other's entire personality via the Staff Picks section. It's cheating. It's also wildly effective.
