DC Winter Date Ideas: Cozy, Warm, and Not Another Movie Night

Winter in DC is objectively short. It is also subjectively endless. Here's how to date through it without resorting to "wanna come over and watch a movie" for the eleventh time.

Ice skating at the Sculpture Garden

Free to watch, $10 to skate, instant conversation, legal hand-holding.

Maydan

There is a literal open fire in the middle of the restaurant. Dates don't get more winter than this.

The Dabney

Wood-fire cooking, warm dining room, seasonal menu that leans hearty.

Columbia Room

Hidden cocktail bar vibe, dim lighting, expensive but the kind of "once per winter" splurge that's worth it.

Off the Record

The bar under the Hay-Adams. Plush, presidential, feels like a scene from a Sorkin show.

Museums on cold days

Renwick Gallery for art + warmth. Phillips Collection for the "small enough to actually experience it" move.

The sneaky winner: a hot chocolate crawl

Dolcezza → Un Je Ne Sais Quoi → Baked & Wired. Walk between them. Pretend you're in a Nora Ephron movie.
