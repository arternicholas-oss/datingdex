Our take
Doi Moi is a great first date spot if you want to look like you tried — without looking like you're trying too hard. The lighting is warm, the menu is interesting enough to give you something to talk about, and the noise level sits right in the sweet spot: lively but not loud. You'll leave looking thoughtful, not desperate.
